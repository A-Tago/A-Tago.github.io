Tago Lab. works for promoting scientific International Relations studies by using large-N data, quantitative text analysis, survey experiments. Please take a look at Atsushi Tago's CV (English) for detail of the activities. To understand Tago Lab.'s scholarly impact, please go to "Atsushi Tago - Google Scholar Citations".
